The Combat in Dark Souls

The combat in Dark Souls can be sepperated into 3 categories: Melee, Ranged and Magic.

Melee is your standard close combat weapons, like swords, maces, axes and shields. It is the most varied group due to the many movesets the different weapons have.

Ranged is the smallest of the groups and is more used as an utility to try and get only one enemy from a group to attack you. The weapons are bow, greatbows and crossbows.

Magic is consists of 3, allthough some might say 4, types of magic: Pyromancies, Miracles, Sorceries. Each type of magic has its own positives and negatives. Pyromancies can be used by all characters, but have a short range. Miracles are really good as utility spells, but are kind of weak when it comes to damage. Sorceries can do a lot of damage, but most of the good spells are only available in the later parts of the game. Both Miracles and Sorceries also require a lot of stat investment in either Intelligence of Faith.
