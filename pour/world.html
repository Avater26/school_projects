The World of Dark Souls

Dark Souls takes place in a medival fantasy world called Lordran. The intire world of Dark Souls is designed to be depressing and dark in order to make the player feel unwelcome in the world.

From a gameplay perspective, most of the game is build on top of itself. The game also has several shortcuts placed around the world, that tend to lead you back to previously explored areas.
