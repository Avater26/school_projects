What is Dark Souls?

Dark Souls is an action RPG made by FromSoftware, which was released between 2011 and 2012 for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360 and PC. A remastered version was later released in 2018 for PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC

The game has become wildly online for being a hard, but fair game where if you die in the game it is most likely your own fault.
